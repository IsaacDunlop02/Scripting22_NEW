Geography

The location of Lethbridge means that it is close to some world-class ski resorts in the Rockies, national parks, the beautiful city of Calgary and the United States border. Chinook winds can bring some of the warmest winters in the western provinces, there are plenty of opportunities to enjoy and experience all the things this city has to offer.
